About Me

Who I am and what drives me

My name is Jordan Morrison and I am a pre-accounting student at Brigham Young University. I enjoy working in environments where attention to detail, organization, and teamwork matter.

I recently returned from a two-year mission for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in Honduras, where I learned Spanish, leadership skills, and how to work with people from many different backgrounds.

I have worked in customer service, food service, and manual labor roles, which helped me develop strong communication skills, responsibility, and the ability to perform under pressure.

My goal is to graduate from BYU in accounting and pursue a career in business or finance where I can continue to grow professionally and serve others.
